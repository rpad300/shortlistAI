## 1. Introduction

ShortlistAI ("we", "us", "our") operates a CV analysis platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

**For All Users:**
- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Country

**For Interviewers:**
- Company name (optional)
- Job posting content
- CVs of candidates (you must have permission to upload these)

**For Candidates:**
- Your CV
- Job posting you're applying for

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Device information
- Usage patterns and analytics
- Cookies and similar technologies

### 2.3 AI-Generated Information

- Structured data extracted from CVs
- Analysis results (scores, strengths, gaps, questions)
- Recommendations and interview preparation content

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Primary Purposes

- **Provide the Service**: Analyze CVs and provide insights
- **Improve Quality**: Refine AI models and prompts
- **Headhunting**: Match candidates to future opportunities
- **Communication**: Send analysis results and relevant opportunities
- **Support**: Respond to inquiries and issues

### 3.2 Legal Bases

We process your data based on:

- **Consent**: You explicitly consent when using the Platform
- **Legitimate Interests**: Improving service quality and headhunting
- **Contract**: To provide the service you requested

## 4. AI Processing and Third-Party Services

### 4.1 AI Providers

Your data is processed by external AI providers:

- **Google Gemini** - Text analysis and generation
- **OpenAI** - GPT models for advanced analysis
- **Anthropic Claude** - Natural language processing
- **Kimi** - Alternative AI processing
- **Minimax** - Alternative AI processing

### 4.2 What We Send to AI Providers

- Job posting text
- CV text (extracted from uploaded files)
- Analysis prompts and context

### 4.3 What We DO NOT Send to AI

- Passwords or authentication credentials
- Payment information
- API keys or system secrets
- Highly sensitive personal data beyond what's in CVs

### 4.4 AI Provider Data Usage

Each AI provider has their own data usage policies. We configure our integrations to:

- Opt-out of training where possible
- Minimize data retention by providers
- Use enterprise or API tiers with better privacy protections where available

## 5. Data Storage and Security

### 5.1 Where Data is Stored

- **Database**: Supabase PostgreSQL (EU region: eu-west-2, London)
- **Files**: Supabase Storage (same region)
- **Backups**: Automated backups within Supabase infrastructure

### 5.2 Security Measures

- Encryption at rest and in transit (HTTPS, TLS)
- Row Level Security (RLS) on all database tables
- Admin-only access to full database
- Regular security audits and updates
- API rate limiting to prevent abuse

### 5.3 Data Retention

- **Active Data**: Retained indefinitely for headhunting purposes
- **Deleted Data**: Removed from active systems within 30 days of deletion request
- **Backups**: May be retained for up to 90 days for disaster recovery

## 6. Your Rights

Under GDPR and similar data protection laws, you have the right to:

### 6.1 Access
Request a copy of your personal data we hold.

### 6.2 Correction
Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.

### 6.3 Deletion
Request deletion of your data, subject to legal and technical limitations.

### 6.4 Portability
Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

### 6.5 Object
Object to processing of your data for certain purposes.

### 6.6 Withdraw Consent
Withdraw consent at any time (does not affect prior lawful processing).

### 6.7 Lodge Complaint
Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority if you believe your rights are violated.
